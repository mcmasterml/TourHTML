Content Sectioning - Semantic HTML

How tags are used to structure and identify sections of content

  • <address>: contact info
  • <article>: self-contained (independent) content
  • <aside>: indirectly-related content
  • <footer>: footer for nearest ancestor
  • <header>: introductory content / navigational aids (heading, logo, search form, author name, etc.)
  • <h1> thru <h6>: levels of section headings
  • <hgroup>: heading group (e.g. a main heading and a sub heading)
  • <main>: dominant content of a the <body> of a document
  • <nav>: navbar / navigation links
  • <section>: generic standalone section (when a more specific tag does not exist, always use a heading element within)
  • <search>: a set of form controls, search, or filtering operations
